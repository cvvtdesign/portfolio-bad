March for Science Sacramento

For this assignment, my challenge was to design a poster that would entice people to join the March for Science in Sacramento. A huge aspect of the walk I wanted to emphasize in my design was that it took place on May 4th, also known as Star Wars day, and they welcomed wearing Star Wars themed costumes to the march. Because of that, I referenced an old Star Wars poster from Japan that shows Luke Skywalker holding his lightsaber in a similar pose to the image I made of the hands holding the sign with March for Science Sacramento's logo.
