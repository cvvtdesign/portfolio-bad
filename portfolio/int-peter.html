Peter and the Starcatcher



I was so honored to be one of the stitchers and dressers for the Theatre and Dance winter quarter production Peter and the Starcatcher (please check out the website!). It was amazing working with the most hardworking and kindest team to build over 30+ costumes and figuring it out how to get them on and off the actors during quick changes. Because it was such an energetic show, our biggest challenge was keeping track of all the tiny accessories and fixing all of the rips and tears before the next scene.
